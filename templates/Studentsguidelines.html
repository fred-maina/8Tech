How To Replace Lost Id

STEP 1

Go to Huduma Centre Room 7 BSSC,to report that your ID is lost.

STEP 2

Write a letter addressed to the registrar academics requesting for replacement of student ID.

To,
Registrar academic
Kenyatta University
P.O box 43844-00100
Nairobi

Through,
Directorate student affairs
Kenyatta university

Through,
Director, security services
Kenyatta university

STEP 3

Payment for the replacement which will be as follows:
- Kshs 700 to replace lost student ID.
- Kshs 500 to replace defaced/expired student ID.
Make payments through the official KU Banks(Family, Equity,National, Co-operative)

STEP 4

Get an approval of the process at BSSC room 7.

STEP 5

Take the letter to the Director of Students affairs,BSSC Room 124/125/126/131 and security office for stamping and signing.

STEP 6

Take the bank slip to finance office cashier and obtain KU official receipt that reflects the amount paid.

STEP 7

Attach that receipt to the letter and take it to the smartcard office(Students Computer Lab rooms 104/105) for replacement.

PROCEDURE FOR CALLING OFF A SEMESTER

To defer the Semester, you need to write an official letter to the Office of Academic Registrar requesting to call off.

In the Sender’s section of your letter (top left corner), indicate your Name, Registration Number, School, and Phone Number. You can include the date of sending the letter.

In the Recipient’s Section (skip one line after the date), write “To: The Academic Registrar – Kenyatta University”. Then the reference you may write is “Request for Calling Off the Semester”.

In the body, be succinct and go straight to the point on why you want to call off. Clearly indicate the reason and the Semester you wish to return to in the future.

Keep in mind that it is a Request, not a command. However, most applications for calling off the Semester are usually accepted without much ado.
